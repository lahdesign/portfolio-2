Goliath Hot Sauce

Goliath Hot Sauce is a passion branding project focusing on logotype and a bottle design.

CVPA Arts Lab Identity

CVPA Arts Lab was a 3 day summer camp to simulate a graphic design class prior to college. We had freedom to choose where to gear our projects, and I chose to work on the Lab's identity for the future years.

Munchie: The Food Delivery Robot

This fictional project was developed inside a self-paced & directed class. Munchie is a food delivery desktop & app concept design. The concept includes a home page, order page, order specifications page, and tracking page.

B's Wax Candle Design

The owner of B's Wax approached me to create a label for her all-natural soy candles. The requirement was to incorporate a purple butterfly, which was used on her original label design.

The Willow Project: Environmental Zine

This collaboration was executed with my good friend and peer Therese Gangemi. We were directed to identify an environmental issue, and design based off evidence merged with our feelings on the issue. The signing of the Willow Project was a directly current event, and we jumped at the opportunity of designing for a fresh wound.

Better Day App Concept

Better Day was an app design coming out of the Information Design class.
